Welcome to COMP2911 Acceptance Tests

This is the *official acceptance test page for COMP2911 at the Univesity Of New South Wales. Here you can download the latest release of the acceptance tests for your major work in the course above. If you have any issues downloading, installing or implementing the testing framework please contact us as outlined below. You can *earn strawberry cupcakes if you are especially awesome.

Support or Contact

Are you having trouble getting the acceptance tests running? No problem, your first point of contact should be to create a comment or a page listing "the magic string" listed on the acceptance test page. If you cannot access that page because your browser is allergic to javascript (elinks!) you can just send a email suggestion to:

bwright (at) cse.unsw.ed.au with the subject line STRAWBERRY CUPCAKES in the header and I will cc it to everyone in the acceptance testing community.
